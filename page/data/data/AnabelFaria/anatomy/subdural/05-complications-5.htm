Question

What is the consequence of the displacement of the brain in this case?

Select the answer you think is correct.

a. no change
b. herniation of the medial aspect of the left temporal lobe (right side of picture)
c. herniation of the parahippocampal gyrus of the right temporal lobe (left side of picture)
Sorry, that’s not right. The consequence in this case is herniation of the parahippocampal gyrus of the right temporal lobe. There is a groove imprinted on the parahippocampal gyrus on the medial aspect of the right temporal lobe (left side of the picture). The increased lateral pressure on the mid-brain has caused venous haemorrhage into the mid-brain seen as dark patches of blood. This was almost certainly a fatal brain stem haemorrhage.
