2.3 Anatomy relevant to subdural haematoma 11/12
Help References

  • The pia mater is a thin layer (approximately one cell thick) that invests the brain, descending into the sulci.
  • The pia mater reflects from the surface of the brain to cover arteries as they enter the brain.
  • There is no pial sheath around veins.
