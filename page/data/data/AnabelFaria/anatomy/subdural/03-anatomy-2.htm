2.3 Anatomy relevant to subdural haematoma 2/12

Help References

The dura mater is fibrous and consists of an outer periosteal layer and an inner meningeal layer. The periosteal layer is the actual periosteum of the skull. The meningeal layer is continuous with the spinal dura mater.
