The image above shows the base of the brain of a man who fell and hit the back of his head on a concrete block. There is a lesion under the frontal lobes at the top of the picture, particularly on the left. What do you think this lesion is?

Select the answer you think is correct.

a. cerebral contusion with leakage of blood into the subdural space

b. tumour

c. meningitis
That’s right. The image shows a cerebral contusion with leakage of blood into the subdural space. The patient has suffered a severe contre-coup injury to the inferior surfaces of the frontal lobe and blood has leaked from the contusion into the subdural space. This is one cause of a subdural haematoma, whereas the subdural haematoma in the previous question is probably due to the tearing of the bridging veins as there is no evidence of contusion on the surface of the brain.
